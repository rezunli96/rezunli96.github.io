Research

My interests lie on the interfaces between AI, economics, and complex systems. My present work focuses on reinterpreting/reconstructing classical economic theories via AI and computation.

I devote to building intelligent software agents in games and real-world market systems by assembling universal game-/decision-theoretical principles distributed across different domains.
